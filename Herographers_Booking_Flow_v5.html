Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book a photographer? ▾
Tap "Book" in the bottom nav, choose your genre, location, date, and tier. We'll match you with a vetted photographer within minutes. You can also browse the marketplace to choose a specific Hero Pro.
When will I receive my photos? ▾
Photos are delivered within 48 hours of your shoot. With Express Delivery (+RM49), you get them in 24 hours. You'll get a notification when your gallery is ready.
What's your cancellation policy? ▾
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before. 50% refund within 24-48 hours of shoot. No refund within 24 hours. See the full policy when you open the Cancel Booking flow.
How are photographers vetted? ▾
All Hero Pro photographers go through portfolio review, ID verification, and a test shoot. We only accept the top 10% of applicants. Look for the ⭐ VETTED badge on profiles.
Can I get a refund if I'm not happy? ▾
Yes! We offer a Quality Guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact support within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or rebooking with another photographer.
Can I order prints of my photos? ▾
Absolutely. Open any gallery in My Bookings, tap "Order Prints", choose size (4×6 to canvas), paper finish, and quantity. Delivery is 5-7 business days.
